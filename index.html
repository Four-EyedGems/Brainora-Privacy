Privacy Policy

Brainora — Offline Brain & Puzzle Games
Last updated: April 5, 2026

Brainora does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. Brainora does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network requests.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Brainora saves the following data locally on your device to provide the game experience:

  • Game progress, scores, and high scores
  • Game settings and preferences (theme, haptics, sound)
  • Daily challenge completion history and streaks
  • Per-game statistics (games played, accuracy, best times)

This data is stored using on-device storage (MMKV) and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

3. Internet Access

Brainora does not require or use an internet connection. The app is fully functional in airplane mode. No data is sent or received over the network.

4. Third-Party Services

Brainora does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media SDKs
  • No tracking or attribution services

5. Advertising

Brainora contains no advertisements of any kind.

6. In-App Purchases

Brainora contains no in-app purchases. All content is available for free.

7. Children's Privacy

Brainora does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations by design, as no personal information is collected.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Stats" option in the app's Settings screen
  • Clearing the app's data through your device's system settings
  • Uninstalling the app (all local data is removed)

9. Permissions

Brainora requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, files, or any other sensitive device features.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
